What Is Ceramic Coating?

Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer that chemically bonds to your vehicle's factory paint, creating a semi-permanent layer of protection. Unlike wax (which lasts 4-6 weeks) or sealant (3-6 months), a professional ceramic coating lasts 2 to 5+ years with proper maintenance.

At Kurtz Auto Detailing, we use Gyeon ceramic coatings — the same professional-grade products trusted by high-end detailers worldwide. These are not consumer-grade coatings from Amazon or AutoZone. They require trained application, proper surface preparation, and controlled curing conditions to perform as intended.

Why Bryan-College Station Vehicles Need Ceramic Coating

The Texas climate is brutal on automotive paint. Between the intense UV exposure, summer temperatures exceeding 100°F, pollen season, bird droppings, tree sap from live oaks, and construction dust from the constant growth around Bryan-College Station, your paint takes a beating year-round. A professional ceramic coating protects against all of it.

What Ceramic Coating Protects Against

  • ✓ UV damage & oxidation — prevents paint from fading and chalking under Texas sun
  • ✓ Bird droppings & tree sap — acidic contaminants sit on the coating, not your paint
  • ✓ Water spots & mineral deposits — hydrophobic surface causes water to bead and sheet off
  • ✓ Light scratches & swirl marks — adds a sacrificial layer harder than factory clear coat
  • ✓ Chemical stains & road grime — easier to wash, dirt doesn't bond to the surface

Our Ceramic Coating Process

Ceramic coating is not a spray-and-wipe product. Professional application requires multiple steps and careful attention to detail. Here is what to expect when you book ceramic coating with Kurtz Auto Detailing:

  1. Thorough Decontamination Wash — full foam bath, bug and tar removal, wheel cleaning
  2. Clay Bar Treatment — removes embedded contaminants the wash cannot reach
  3. Iron Fallout Removal — dissolves metallic particles bonded to the paint
  4. Paint Correction (if needed) — removes swirl marks and scratches for a flawless base
  5. Panel Wipe & Surface Prep — strips oils and residues so the coating bonds properly
  6. Ceramic Coating Application — Gyeon coating applied panel by panel, leveled, and cured
  7. Final Inspection — walkthrough with you to verify results

The entire process typically takes a full day. We bring all equipment and products to your location — no need to drop off your vehicle at a shop.
