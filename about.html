About Kurtz Auto Detailing

Founded in Navasota, TX. Built on results, not promises.

Started From a Side Job

Kurtz Auto Detailing was founded by Israel Yanez in May 2024. What started as a side job — detailing cars for friends and family on weekends — quickly turned into something bigger. Word got around. People kept coming back. And within months, what was supposed to be extra income became a full-time business.

Since then, Israel has completed over 300 details and earned more than 100 five-star reviews from clients across the Brazos Valley. Every single job is handled personally by Israel — no subcontractors, no employees you've never met showing up at your door. When you book with Kurtz, you get Israel. That's the point.

The name Kurtz comes from family. The business was built on a simple idea: do great work, be honest about what you can and can't do, and let the results speak for themselves. No hype. No bait-and-switch pricing. No pressure to buy things you don't need.

Proud to Serve the Aggie Community

Bryan-College Station is home to Texas A&M University and one of the most passionate communities in Texas. We're proud to serve Aggies past and present — from students getting their first car detailed before a game day weekend to faculty and staff who want their vehicles looking sharp year-round. Whether you're in Bryan, College Station, Navasota, Brenham, Montgomery County, Conroe, or The Woodlands, Kurtz Auto Detailing comes to you.

With 300+ details completed, 100+ five-star client reviews, and professional Gyeon and Koch-Chemie products in every job, we've earned our reputation one vehicle at a time. Browse our detailing gallery to see the results, or check out our full service menu to find the right package for your vehicle. Ready to book? Request a free quote or call (979) 574-2005.

Professional Products

We use Gyeon and Koch-Chemie — professional-grade products trusted by high-end detailers around the world. These aren't the bottles you'll find at AutoZone or O'Reilly. They're commercial formulations designed for trained professionals, and they deliver results that consumer-grade products simply can't match.

From ceramic coatings to interior cleaners to paint correction compounds, every product we use is chosen for a reason. We don't cut corners with cheap supplies to save a few dollars. Your vehicle deserves better than that, and so do you.
