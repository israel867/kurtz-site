Why Huntsville Trusts Kurtz Auto Detailing

Huntsville is home to Sam Houston State University, the Sam Houston National Forest, and a steady flow of I-45 corridor traffic between Houston and Dallas. Whether you are SHSU faculty, a student keeping your ride in top shape, or a homeowner on a rural property along Highway 75 or FM 1791, your vehicle faces a unique set of challenges in Walker County.

The surrounding forest environment means constant exposure to pollen, tree sap, and road debris — all of which embed in paint and break down your clear coat over time. A professional ceramic coating creates a durable barrier against sap, pollen, UV rays, and bird droppings, keeping your finish protected for 2 to 5+ years with proper maintenance. For vehicles that already show swirl marks, scratches, or oxidation from years of exposure, our paint correction service removes that damage and restores the paint before coating.

With over 300 details completed and 100+ five-star reviews, we have earned our reputation one vehicle at a time. Every detail uses professional-grade Gyeon and Koch-Chemie products. Check our detailing gallery for real results, or read what customers say on our reviews page.
