SMS Terms & Conditions

How our text messaging program works. Short, clear, no fine print tricks.

Effective date: April 14, 2026

When you give us your mobile number — on our quote form, by text, or when you call us — you're agreeing to the terms below. They cover how our SMS program works, what kinds of messages we send, and how to opt out any time.

Consent to Receive Messages

By providing your mobile number to Kurtz Auto Detailing, you consent to receive SMS text messages from us. These may include:

  • Quote responses and pricing info
  • Appointment confirmations and reminders
  • Service updates while we're working on your vehicle
  • Follow-ups after the job is done
  • Occasional promos or seasonal offers

Messages may be sent using automated technology.

Message Frequency

Frequency varies based on your interactions with us. Most customers get around 2–4 messages per month, with a few more during an active quote conversation or while we're scheduling and delivering a service.

Message & Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Kurtz Auto Detailing does not charge you for SMS messages, but your mobile carrier may charge you according to your plan. Check with your carrier if you're not sure.

How to Opt Out

You can cancel at any time. Reply STOP to any text from Kurtz Auto Detailing and you'll be unsubscribed. We'll send one confirmation message, and then you won't get any more. If you ever want to opt back in, just text us or fill out the quote form again.

Help

Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact us directly at (979) 574-2005 or israel@kurtzautodetailing.com.

Supported Carriers

Our SMS program works on all major U.S. carriers, including AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, and others. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Privacy

Your mobile number and SMS messages are handled according to our Privacy Policy. We do not share mobile information or SMS opt-in data with any third parties for marketing or promotional purposes.

Changes to These Terms

We may update these SMS Terms from time to time. Any material changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
