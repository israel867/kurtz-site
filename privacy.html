Privacy Policy

How we handle your information. No legalese. No surprises.

Last updated: April 2, 2026

We believe in transparency — with our pricing, our work, and how we handle your data. This policy explains what we collect, why, and what we do with it. Short version: we use your info to respond to your quote and schedule your appointment. That's it.

What We Collect

When you fill out our quote form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Vehicle information (year, make, model, condition)
  • Any details you include in your message

How We Use It

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your quote request
  • Schedule and confirm appointments
  • Follow up on completed work
  • Answer your questions

We don't use your info for marketing blasts or cold outreach. If you reach out to us, we respond. That's the extent of it.

We Don't Sell Your Data

Period. We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. Your info stays between you and us.

Third-Party Services

We use a few services to run this website. Here's exactly what they are and what they do:

  • Formspark — Processes our quote form submissions. When you submit a quote, Formspark delivers it to us. They don't use your data for anything else. Formspark Privacy Policy
  • Vercel — Hosts this website. They may collect standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited) as part of normal web hosting. Vercel Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics — If enabled, collects anonymous usage data (pages visited, time on site, general location) to help us understand how people use the site. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics. Google Privacy Policy

Cookies

We keep cookies minimal. The only cookies on this site come from standard analytics (if enabled). We don't use tracking cookies for advertising, retargeting, or any of that. No cookie banners because there's nothing shady to warn you about.

Your Rights

Want to know what info we have on you? Want us to delete it? Just ask. Send us an email or give us a call. We'll take care of it. No hoops to jump through.
