Wax gives a warm shine and lasts about 4 to 6 weeks. Sealant is a synthetic protectant that lasts 3 to 6 months with better durability. Ceramic coating is a semi-permanent layer that bonds to your paint and lasts 2 to 5+ years with proper maintenance. Each has its place depending on your budget, how long you want protection to last, and how much maintenance you're willing to do. See our services page for pricing and options. For a deeper dive, check out our blog post on how long ceramic coating actually lasts.

An ozone treatment uses an ozone generator to eliminate odors at the molecular level — smoke, pet smells, mildew, food, you name it. It's not a cover-up like an air freshener. It actually destroys the bacteria and compounds that cause the smell. We offer it as an add-on to any interior service.
