Looking for mobile auto detailing near Texas A&M? Kurtz Auto Detailing is the most trusted mobile detailer in the Bryan-College Station area. Founded by Israel Yanez in May 2024, Kurtz has grown from a weekend side job into the highest-rated mobile detailing business in the Brazos Valley — with over 300 details completed and 100+ five-star client reviews. We serve College Station, Bryan, Navasota, Brenham, Montgomery, Waller, Magnolia, Conroe, The Woodlands, Spring, and Huntsville.

Our most popular services include ceramic coating for long-term paint protection, paint correction to remove swirl marks and scratches, interior deep cleans for vehicles that need a full reset, and our signature full details that cover every inch of your car inside and out. Whether you drive a daily commuter, a truck that sees ranch roads every week, or a weekend car you want to keep showroom-fresh, we have a service package built for your needs, from a quick clay wash and seal up to the full Million Dollar Package. Pricing is custom to your vehicle — get a free quote in under a minute.

Mobile detailing means you do not have to drop your vehicle off at a shop and arrange a ride. Israel brings all water, power, tools, and products directly to your driveway, apartment complex, office parking lot, or wherever your vehicle is parked. You get the same professional-grade results as a brick-and-mortar shop — without the inconvenience. Every job uses Gyeon ceramic coatings and Koch-Chemie compounds and polishes, the same brands trusted by high-end detailers worldwide.

What sets Kurtz apart is consistency. Israel personally handles or oversees every detail — no random subcontractors, no bait-and-switch. The same standard of care on a standard detail as on a full correction and coating. That is why clients across the Brazos Valley, Montgomery County, and the Texas A&M community keep coming back.
